    ENERGY FOR YOU

    As a customer focused technology driven fuels retailing company, ENYO provides best-in-class retail experiences, leveraging complimentary brands and optimized return to investors.

    ENYO was established in 2016, following the growing liberalization and evolution of oil and gas industries in Nigeria and beyond. Our primary focus is to integrate First-Level customer service experience with fuel retailing and renewable energy products in the Nigerian, African and Nationwide markets to create a whole new perspective.

    Supported by established relationships with offices in Lagos, Abuja and Portharcourt, ENYO is well positioned to leverage local and regional expertise in the structuring of the key tenets of the Nigerian fuels retailing industry.

    With dedicated core of highly experienced professionals, ENYO has proven capabilities in every facet of the fuel retailing, energy, technology and consumer business.

    OUR BUSINESS

    ERS is the result of an unrelenting desire to expand our trademark professionalism and expertise in the downstream energy space to the last mile consumer thereby creating one of the most exciting fuels retail brands in the Country.

    In addition, we will leverage the excellence of our partners in the terminal business (FEL), international supply business ENYO Trading (ET) and ERS to create a value chain that is difficult to replicate in the Nigerian market.
